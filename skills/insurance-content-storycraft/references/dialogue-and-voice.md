# Dialogue and Voice

## Core idea

Dialogue is not decoration. It is verbal action.

Good dialogue keeps text and subtext separate. Do not explain every feeling that the line already carries.

## Rewrite rules

- Replace abstract claims with spoken lines.
- Replace preaching with observation.
- Keep subtext whenever possible.
- Let the reader infer part of the meaning.
- Do not say the subtext out loud immediately after a quote.
- Use pauses, short reactions, and concrete next actions instead of interpretation.

## Good source material

- 客户原话
- 你自己的即时回应
- 你提炼出的潜台词

## Client audio insertion

For `出镜 + 客户音频穿插` scripts:

- use the speaker to carry structure
- use client audio to carry reality and state change
- keep each audio clip short
- place audio where a value shift occurs
- do not ask client audio to explain the whole case
- if audio has not been recorded yet, do not fabricate client quotes

### Before audio exists

Write an audio plan instead of fake quotes:

- interview question: what to ask
- selection criteria: what kind of sentence is worth keeping
- story slot: where the clip belongs
- fallback edit: what to do if the sound is unclear or the answer is too polished

Good placeholder:

- `[客户原声候选：问“当时最乱的是哪一步？”；优先保留停顿、犹豫、具体担心；5-10 秒]`

Bad placeholder:

- `[客户：多亏你专业又负责，不然我真不知道怎么办]`

The second version sounds like a testimonial ad and may invent emotional evidence the speaker has not actually given.

### Audio placement test

Before inserting a clip, identify:

- context before audio: what must the viewer know to feel this line?
- audio line: what exact sentence carries the state?
- value shift: what changed before/after this line?
- after audio: can the speaker pause or move to action instead of explaining the meaning?

### Avoid after audio

Avoid lines like:

- 这说明客户当时非常焦虑。
- 这体现了专业顾问的重要性。
- 这就是找对人的价值。

Prefer:

- 我听到这句的时候停了一下。
- 我当时先做的不是给结论。
- 先把这几件事分开。

## Strong patterns

### Micro-dialogue

Use when you need instant realism:

- 她问……
- 我说……

### Reflective line

Use when you need warmth or authority:

- 做这行越久，我越发现……

### Reframe line

Use when you need to shift perspective:

- 真正要紧的不是 X，而是 Y。

## Avoid

- 宣传腔
- 空泛术语
- 一句话说完全部结论
- 高压 CTA
- 把潜台词翻译成旁白
- 客户一句话后立刻升华
