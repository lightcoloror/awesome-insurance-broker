# Channel and Topics

## Purpose

Pick the right topic shape for the right channel before drafting.

## Channel matrix

### 朋友圈

- Best for trust, light education, soft conversion, and relationship warmth
- Good topics:
  - 保险知识提醒
  - 误区纠正
  - 咨询/理赔/成交场景
  - 学习成长
  - 生活与职业交叉内容

### 图文 / 公众号 / 小红书

- Best for structured explanation
- Good formats:
  - 攻略
  - 科普
  - 避坑
  - 教程
  - 合集

### 短视频

- Best for single-point clarity and emotional capture
- Good formats:
  - 口播
  - 场景剧
  - Vlog
  - 热点解读

### 直播

- Best for deeper trust and conversion
- Topic classes:
  - 流量话题
  - 专业话题
  - 人设话题

## Topic filters

For every topic, answer:

1. Who is it for?
2. What pain point does it solve?
3. What is the best channel?
4. What is the target action?
